About Us
EvaGroup is a media brand, with a focus on innovation in technology, leadership, world changing ideas, creativity, and design. Written for and about the most progressive business leaders, EvaGroup inspires readers to think expansively, lead with purpose, embrace change, and shape the future of business.
Launched in May 2024 by Mahissa Salehnezhad and Rahimjon Berdiev, two EMU students, EvaGroup blog was founded on a single premise: A global revolution was changing business, and business was changing the world. Discarding the old rules of business, EvaGroup set out to chronicle how changing companies create and compete, to highlight new business practices, and to showcase the teams and individuals who are inventing the future and reinventing business.
